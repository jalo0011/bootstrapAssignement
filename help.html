6 Tips For You

Make Suggestions

Be active in your community and suggest plans that can be put into action in your community to reduce your carbon footprint.

tip #1

Limit driving

If possible, find ways to limit your use of cars. Find a means of alternate transportation that doesn't pollute.

tip #2

Limit your use of electricity

Simply try to use less electricity throughout the day.

tip #3

Don't avoid the issue!

Talk to other's about the issue. Educate yourself on ways that you can limit the pollution that you cause.

tip #4

Plant a tree

Just do it.

tip #5

Limit personal pollution

Avoid buying items that pollute when there are alternatives that are biodegratable or use natural energy.
